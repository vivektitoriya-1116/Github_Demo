Our Story

Started with a Notebook, Built with Love

Paperie & Co. was founded in 2018 by two lifelong journal keepers who couldn't find stationery that was both beautiful and genuinely useful. What started as a small curated shop in Mumbai has grown into a community of over 40,000 thoughtful writers across India.

Every item in our catalogue has been personally tested. If we wouldn't reach for it on our own desks, it doesn't earn a place in our shop. We source from independent papermakers, family-run nib workshops, and sustainable material producers — people who believe, as we do, that making something by hand is a kind of love.
